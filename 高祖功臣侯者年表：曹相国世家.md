\phi(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{x})=\sum_{j_{1}, j_{2} \in \mathcal{C}_{2}}\left\langle\mathbf{w}_{j_{1}, f_{2}}, \mathbf{w}_{j_{2}, f_{1}}\right\rangle x_{j_{1}} x_{j_{2}}
$$
